PHY 259 - Advanced Properties of Motion

Abstract

How do you analyze a block on a ramp? Do you use equations? A computer? In this class we go beyond physics lab and fully model properties of motion using statistical analysis and quantum field theory from Star Trek.

Details

  • Instructor: Richard Fenyman
  • Class Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 11:00 am to 12:30 pm in Alderman 210
  • Materials: Textbook (Elements of Motion by Feline Physics Press) and handouts to be provided.
  • Syllabus: [PDF]

Prerequisites

You need a strong grasp of Physics and Computer Programming. In addition, the following classes are strongly suggested:

  • PHY 101 - Fundamentals of Mechanics
  • PHY 102 - Fundamentals of Electronics
  • PHY 201 - Quantum Mechanics
